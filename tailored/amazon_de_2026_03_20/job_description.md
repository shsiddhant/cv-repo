# Data Engineer - Amazon
Job ID: 3203439 | ADCI - Karnataka

## Description

### About the Role

We are looking for a creative, dynamic and motivated Data Engineer to join the Trustworthy Shopping Experience(TSE) Operations team. The mission of this team is to protect Customer trust on Amazon’s stores world wide by using statistical analysis.
The ideal candidate thrives in working with large and complex data warehouse environments and has excellent verbal and written communication skills, is motivated by developing innovative and simplified solutions to solve complex analytical problems using very large data sets.
The candidate is passionate about working with large data sets and bringing data together to answer business questions and drive change.
These analysis and solutions will be fundamental to finding opportunities and creating new programs aimed at helping arrest abusive / bad actors on amazon platform faster.
He/she will have a unique opportunity to closely partner with senior leaders and product, science, technology and operations teams across the organization driving programs that are critical to protecting our Buyers and genuine Sellers from bad actors.
He/she will also play a critical role in influencing and shaping organization's strategy and product roadmap.



### Key job responsibilities

- Architecture design and implementation of next generation data pipelines and BI solutions
- Manage AWS resources including EC2, RDS, Redshift, Kinesis, EMR, Lambda, Glue, Apache Airflow etc.
- Build and deliver high quality data architecture and pipelines to support business analyst, data scientists, and customer reporting needs.
- Interface with other technology teams to extract, transform, and load data from a wide variety of data sources
- Continually improve ongoing reporting and analysis processes, automating or simplifying self-service support for customers

### Basic Qualifications

- 1+ years of data engineering experience
- Experience with SQL
- Experience with data modeling, warehousing and building ETL pipelines
- Experience with one or more query language (e.g., SQL, PL/SQL, DDL, MDX, HiveQL, SparkSQL, Scala)
- Experience with one or more scripting language (e.g., Python, KornShell)

### Preferred Qualifications

- Experience with big data technologies such as: Hadoop, Hive, Spark, EMR
